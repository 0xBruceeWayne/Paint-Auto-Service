How does the AI work in the booking flow?

This is the core UX — how the user actually talks to the AI to get their trip booked.

A

Conversational Chat

User types naturally: "I want a 5-day trip to Bali in July under $3000." AI responds with options, asks follow-ups, and books in the chat window. Claude/GPT-style interface embedded in the site.

B

Smart Search + AI Layer

Traditional search bar with destination, dates, budget — but AI auto-fills, suggests alternatives, and surfaces personalised bundles. AI lives in the sidebar/overlay, not the center.

C

AI-First Wizard

The site opens with a full-screen AI prompt: "Where do you want to go?" — then guides the user step-by-step (destination → dates → vibe → budget → review → book). No traditional search at all.
