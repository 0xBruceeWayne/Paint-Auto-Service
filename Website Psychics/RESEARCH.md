## 1. MARKET OVERVIEW

### Size & Growth
- **US psychic services market:** $2.3–2.4 billion (2024–2025), growing ~5.5% CAGR
- **Global online psychic reading market:** $6.8B (2025) → $14.2B by 2034 (8.5% CAGR)
- **103,000 psychic service businesses** in the US (fragmented — no single player holds >5% share)
- Online psychic reading market growing at **11.8% CAGR** (faster than offline)
- "Psychic" keyword: ~$44 average Google AdWords spend per search (high commercial intent)
- $40M+ spent annually on astrology apps (up from $12M in 2016)

### Who Buys
- **22% of Americans** have consulted a psychic/fortune-teller/medium
- **41% of Americans** believe in psychics; **24%** believe people can see the future
- **Gender:** 67% female globally; women 69% vs men 55% embrace new age beliefs
- **Age:** Primary paying demographic = **women 25–54**. Male clients: 27–54
- **Gen Z & Millennials** are the fastest-growing segment — mobile-first, app-native
- **Frequency:** Most clients use "less than once a month" but ~400K use "several times a week"
- **Top reasons people seek readings:** Love/relationships (#1 by far), career, grief/loss, life direction, family conflict

| Type | Share |
|------|-------|
| General (love, life, career) | 45% |

## 3. BUSINESS MODEL

### Revenue Streams (Stack All of These)
1. **Pay-per-minute sessions** — Core revenue. $1–$30/min depending on advisor tier.
   - Platform takes 30–50% cut from each session
   - Advisors set their own rates within platform min/max
2. **Subscription packages** — Fixed monthly fee for bundle of minutes/sessions
   - Subscription model is **projected to dominate** market going forward
   - In-app subscription tiers with bundled minutes, discounted per-min rates
3. **Intro offers** — First 3 minutes free / $1 for 5 minutes / 50% off first reading
   - Highest conversion driver. Essential. Non-negotiable.
4. **Package deals** — Buy 3 sessions for $X (discount incentive, increases LTV)
5. **Tips / Gifts** — Clients tip readers after sessions (Oranum has virtual gift economy)
6. **Premium membership** — Priority advisor access, exclusive readers, better rates
7. **Affiliate program** — Pay affiliates $100–$150 per converted new client
   - Kasamba: $125/client. Keen: $100/client. Psychic Oz: up to $150/client. Cookie: 30–90 days.
8. **Content / SEO flywheel** — Horoscopes, tarot content, birth charts → organic traffic → reading conversions

### Typical Rates
| Advisor Level | Per-minute rate |
|---------------|----------------|
| New / intro | $1–$3/min |
| Standard | $3–$8/min |
| Senior / Premium | $8–$15/min |
| Elite / Master | $15–$30/min |

### Key Metrics to Benchmark Against
- Average session: 15–20 minutes
- Average spend per session (standard tier): $50–$100
- LTV of returning client: HIGH — repeat usage is core to model
- CAC via affiliate: $100–$150
- Intro offer conversion: target 40%+ of trials → paid session

## 5. READER/ADVISOR MANAGEMENT

### Vetting Process (What Top Platforms Do)
1. Application with credentials/background
2. Live trial reading with expert reviewers
3. Accuracy and ethical assessments
4. Background verification
5. Ongoing performance monitoring (ratings, session reviews)
6. Acceptance rates: California Psychics = 2%, Psychic Source ≈ 5%

### Reader Profile — What to Show
- Photo (professional, authentic)
- Short video intro (major differentiator — almost no platform does this well)
- Specialties (love, career, grief, life path, etc.)
- Reading tools used (tarot, crystal ball, astrology, clairvoyance, etc.)
- Style description (empathetic? direct? spiritual vs practical?)
- Years of experience
- Session count + star rating + review count
- Price per minute
- Availability status (online now / scheduled / offline)
- Next available slot if offline
- Language(s) spoken

### Reader Tools Needed
- Earnings dashboard
- Availability calendar (set hours)
- Session history
- Client notes/CRM (remember returning clients)
- Mobile app with push when client books
- Analytics (ratings, earnings, session stats)

## 6. TECHNOLOGY STACK

### Core Infrastructure
| Layer | Options |
|-------|---------|
| **Real-time video/audio** | Twilio Video, Daily.co, Agora, LiveKit (open source) |
| **Real-time chat** | Stream Chat, Pusher, Socket.io |
| **Per-minute billing** | Stripe with metered billing / Twilio + webhook billing |
| **Scheduling** | Calendly API, custom, Cal.com |
| **Push notifications** | Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) |
| **Backend** | Node.js / Next.js API routes / Laravel |
| **Database** | PostgreSQL + Redis (sessions/presence) |
| **Mobile** | React Native or Flutter |
| **Search/filter** | Algolia or Elasticsearch (advisor search) |
| **Payments** | Stripe primary (note: some processors restrict psychic/fortune-telling — need approved Stripe account) |

### AI Opportunities (Differentiators)
- **AI advisor matching** — Analyze client input (question type, mood, budget) → rank best-fit advisors
- **Personalized horoscopes** — LLM-generated daily/weekly based on birth chart, not generic text
- **Session summaries** — AI-generated post-reading summary sent to client
- **Recommendation engine** — "Clients like you loved this advisor"
- **AI-powered content** — Dynamic horoscope content for SEO at scale
- **Smart intake form** — Before booking: AI asks a few questions to understand the client's situation better, shows matched readers

### Per-Minute Billing Logic (Critical)
1. Session starts → timer starts → webhook fires every 60 seconds
2. Stripe metered billing OR pre-authorize card then charge on session end
3. If client card fails mid-session → graceful end with warning
4. Build credit wallet system (buy credits upfront → spend during sessions) — reduces billing friction and increases session frequency

## 7. UX & DESIGN STRATEGY

### What Currently Exists (The Old Standard)
- Dark purple/navy/gold color scheme — overdone, feels cheap
- Stock photos of crystals, candles, moons
- Grid of advisor cards with star ratings
- Banner ads and pop-ups everywhere
- No visual hierarchy — everything screams at once
- Desktop-first design from 2012

### The New Standard (What To Build)
- **Dark, premium, cinematic** — think luxury brand, not carnival fortune teller
- **Deep space / midnight mystical palette** — deep navy, obsidian, iridescent gold, soft glow accents
- **3D hero elements** — floating orb, tarot cards, constellation map (Three.js)
- **Liquid Glass cards** for advisor profiles — iOS 26 glassmorphism treatment
- **Animated backgrounds** — subtle particle field, slow nebula drift, constellation lines
- **Cinematic video intros** — reader profile videos auto-play on hover
- **Personalized onboarding flow** — 3-question quiz before showing advisors
- **Scroll-driven reveal animations** — GSAP ScrollTrigger, each section dramatic entrance

### Homepage Structure (Conversion-Optimized)
1. **Hero** — "Get your reading now" CTA above fold. Floating 3D orb/tarot. Mood atmospheric.
2. **Intro offer** — Prominent: "First 3 minutes free" banner (never buried)
3. **Reader spotlight** — 3–5 featured advisors, video thumbnails, live status indicators
4. **How it works** — 3 steps, animated
5. **Testimonials** — Video testimonials >> text
6. **Reading types** — Visual cards: Love, Career, Tarot, Astrology, etc.
7. **Trust section** — "3M+ readings completed", "Money-back guarantee", "Verified advisors"
8. **Content section** — Today's horoscope, tarot card of the day (SEO + engagement)
9. **CTA footer** — Persistent "Start your free reading" floating button

### Advisor Browsing Page
- Filters: specialty, reading type (chat/phone/video), price range, availability, language, rating, style
- Card shows: photo, name, specialty, price, rating, session count, "Online now" badge
- Hover → short video auto-plays (30-sec intro)
- Quick-book button without needing to go to full profile
- AI recommendation row: "Best match for your question"

### Onboarding Flow (3 Questions Before Showing Advisors)
1. "What would you like guidance on?" (Love / Career / Family / Life Path / Grief / Other)
2. "What type of reading feels right?" (Chat / Phone / Video)
3. "What's your budget?" ($1–5/min / $5–10/min / $10+/min)
→ Show matched advisors + "Here are your top 3 matches"

## 8. TRUST & CREDIBILITY

### Essential Trust Signals
- Money-back guarantee (or credit guarantee) — **mandatory**
- Advisor acceptance rate stat ("only 3 in 100 advisors accepted")
- Total readings completed counter (live/animated)
- Years in operation
- Verified payment badges
- SSL / privacy-first messaging
- "Your privacy is protected" — discretion is a key purchase driver
- No judgment language — many clients feel embarrassed; make them feel safe

### Review System
- Verified reviews only (session must have occurred)
- Star rating + written review + session type
- Response from advisor option
- Filter by: most recent, highest rated, verified only
- Show review count prominently (social proof)

### Legal — Non-Negotiable
- "For entertainment purposes only" disclaimer on every page footer
- "Not a substitute for professional medical, legal, or financial advice"
- 18+ age requirement with acknowledgment on checkout
- Banned in: Oklahoma, Wisconsin, Minnesota, North Carolina (for-profit fortune telling)
- Restricted in: Pennsylvania, New York (must be entertainment only, not predictive claims)
- Privacy policy: GDPR compliant, explicit about session data (recordings? logs?)
- Payment processor: Get Stripe explicitly approved for "entertainment/spiritual services" category

## 11. THE COMPETITIVE GAPS — Your Differentiation Targets

These are things **no platform does well right now**. This is where you win:

### 1. Modern 3D / Cinematic Design
No psychic platform has a premium visual experience. They all look dated. A Three.js immersive hero with particle cosmos, animated tarot cards, liquid glass profiles — immediately sets you apart before a user reads a single word.

### 2. Video-First Advisor Profiles
30-second auto-play video intro on hover is standard in marketplaces (Airbnb, Airbnb Experiences) but **zero major psychic platforms do it**. This is the single biggest trust-building opportunity.

### 3. AI-Powered Advisor Matching
Onboarding quiz → AI scores all advisors → shows ranked matches with explanation ("This advisor specializes in exactly your situation"). Removes the paralysis of a grid of 900 profiles.

### 4. Personalized Dynamic Horoscopes
LLM-generated horoscopes that are actually personalized (birth chart + current planetary positions). Not the same generic text for 1/12 of humanity. This alone drives massive organic traffic and app engagement.

### 5. Post-Session Summary
AI-generated summary of key themes from your reading (based on chat log if text session). Sent to client email. Builds retention and perceived value.

### 6. Community Layer
Forum/community where clients share experiences, ask questions, discuss zodiac signs. Drives retention and organic content. Zero platforms have this at quality.

### 7. Transparency Dashboard
Show advisor acceptance rate, average client satisfaction by advisor, flagging history, session cancellation rate. Radical transparency vs competitors who hide everything.

### 8. Premium Subscription Tier
Monthly subscription: X minutes included, discounted overage rate, access to exclusive top-tier advisors, priority booking, monthly one-on-one check-in reading. SaaS-style recurring revenue on top of per-minute.
